To team members:

To ensure that each member is assigned different posts, enter the following address in the URL bar: http://aoi.naist.jp/annotation/Total members/Your number
For example, if our group has 5 members and my number is 3, then http://aoi.naist.jp/annotation/5/3 is my assignment
Enter http://aoi.naist.jp/annotation/0 to check all posts
Enter http://aoi.naist.jp/annotation/sample to see samples
